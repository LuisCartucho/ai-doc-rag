RETURN POLICY – NORDIC SHOP LTD
Company No: 12345678
Valid from: January 1, 2024
Last updated: December 15, 2023

1. RIGHT OF WITHDRAWAL
Consumers have a 14-day right of withdrawal from the date the item is received, pursuant to the Consumer Contracts Act § 18.
The withdrawal period only applies to unused items in original, undamaged packaging.
The right of withdrawal lapses for digital products that have been activated or downloaded.

2. RETURNING ITEMS
Items are returned to the following address:
  Nordic Shop Ltd
  Warehouse Road 12
  8000 Aarhus C

Returns are free for complaints and items delivered in error.
In case of withdrawal, the customer pays return shipping (£3.99 via GLS pickup point).

The customer must contact customer service at returns@nordicshop.com to receive a return label.
The item must be dispatched before the withdrawal period expires.

3. RIGHT OF COMPLAINT
The statutory 2-year right of complaint applies to all items under the Sale of Goods Act.
A complaint must be filed no later than 2 months after the defect is discovered.
Complaints are filed via email at complaints@nordicshop.com or through the customer portal.

4. REFUNDS
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 14 days of us receiving the returned item.
Shipping costs are not refunded upon withdrawal (only in case of complaints).

5. EXCEPTIONS – ITEMS THAT CANNOT BE RETURNED
The following categories of items are exempt from the right of withdrawal:
- Custom-made items produced to the customer's specifications
- Digital products (software, licenses) after activation
- Hygiene products and underwear that have been used or unpacked
- Fresh food and perishable items
- Sealed audio and video recordings that have been opened
