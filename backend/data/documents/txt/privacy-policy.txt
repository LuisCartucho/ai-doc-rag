Last updated: February 1, 2024

1. DATA CONTROLLER
Nordic Shop Ltd is the data controller for the processing of your personal data in connection with your purchase and use of our website.
Contact regarding data protection: privacy@nordicshop.com

2. WHAT PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT
We collect the following information:
- Name and address (for delivery and invoicing)
- Email address (for order confirmation and newsletter, if you have given consent)
- Phone number (for delivery notifications from the courier)
- Payment data (card details are processed by Stripe – we do not store your card data)
- IP address and browser data (via necessary and analytical cookies)
- Order history and preferences (for customer service and improving our services)

3. PURPOSE AND LEGAL BASIS
We process your data for the following purposes:
- Order processing and delivery (performance of contract, GDPR art. 6(1)(b))
- Customer service and support (legitimate interest, GDPR art. 6(1)(f))
- Marketing and newsletter (consent, GDPR art. 6(1)(a))
- Statutory bookkeeping and accounting (legal obligation, GDPR art. 6(1)(c))
- Fraud prevention (legitimate interest, GDPR art. 6(1)(f))

4. RETENTION AND DELETION
Order data and invoice details are retained for 5 years (required under the Bookkeeping Act).
Marketing data and consent preferences are deleted immediately upon withdrawal of consent.
Cookie data is deleted after 13 months.
Inactive customer accounts are deleted after 3 years of inactivity.

5. SHARING OF DATA
We only share your data with:
- Courier companies (GLS, DAO) – for delivery of your order
- Payment processor (Stripe) – for secure payment
- Accounting software (Xero) – for bookkeeping
We never sell your personal data to third parties.

6. YOUR RIGHTS
You have the following rights under GDPR:
- Right of access to your personal data
- Right to rectification of incorrect information
- Right to erasure ("the right to be forgotten")
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
Requests are sent to: privacy@nordicshop.com

7. COMPLAINTS
You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO):
  ico.org.uk | 0303 123 1113

8. COOKIES
We use necessary cookies for the website to function.
With your consent, we also use analytical cookies (Google Analytics).
You can withdraw your consent at any time via our cookie settings.
