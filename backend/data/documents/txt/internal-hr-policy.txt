INTERNAL HR POLICY – NORDIC SHOP LTD
CONFIDENTIAL – For internal use only
Valid from: January 1, 2024
Approved by: HR Director

1. EMPLOYMENT CONDITIONS
All employees are covered by applicable employment law and the relevant collective agreement.
Probationary period: 3 months for all positions (may be extended to 6 months by agreement).
Employment contract is issued no later than 1 month after the start of employment.

2. WORKING HOURS AND FLEXIBLE WORKING
Standard working week: 37 hours including ½ hour paid lunch break.
Flexible working: Core hours 9:00 AM–3:00 PM. Flexible presence 7:00–9:00 AM and 3:00–5:00 PM.
Overtime is compensated with time off in lieu 1:1 within 3 months, or with an overtime supplement per collective agreement.
Remote work: Max 2 days per week by agreement with the immediate manager (applies after probationary period).

3. HOLIDAY AND DAYS OFF
Annual leave: 25 days paid holiday per year.
Senior days off: 5 extra days from the calendar year in which the employee turns 60.
Care days: 2 paid care days per child under 14 per year.
Christmas party and summer party are held with full pay compensation.
Christmas Eve (December 24) is a paid day off.

4. SICKNESS AND ABSENCE
Sickness must be reported to the immediate manager no later than 9:00 AM on the first day of illness by phone.
The first sick day is paid.
For illness exceeding 3 consecutive days, a doctor's certificate is required (paid by the employer).
Return to work must also be reported to the immediate manager.

5. EMPLOYEE DEVELOPMENT AND TRAINING
All employees are entitled to at least 1 annual performance review (APR) per year.
Training budget: Up to £1,000 per employee per year, requires management approval.
Courses and training during working hours are paid at full salary.
External education (e.g. diploma or master's degree) requires an individual agreement with HR.

6. SALARY AND BENEFITS
Salary is paid on the 25th of the month (or the nearest banking day).
Salary negotiation takes place once a year in January.
Employee benefit: 15% discount on all Nordic Shop products (max £500 per year).
Health insurance is offered to all full-time employees after 6 months.
Pension contribution: 8% of salary (employer pays 5.6%, employee pays 2.4%).

7. CODE OF CONDUCT
All employees are expected to behave professionally and respectfully.
Harassment and discrimination are not tolerated and will result in immediate disciplinary action.
Confidential information about customers and colleagues must not be shared externally.
