1. WARRANTY PERIOD
All electronics (computers, phones, tablets, powered accessories) are covered by a 2-year warranty from the date of purchase.
Mechanical accessories and consumables (cables, bags, covers) are covered by a 1-year warranty.
Batteries are covered by a 6-month warranty for capacity loss exceeding 20%.

2. WHAT THE WARRANTY COVERS
The warranty covers manufacturing defects and material defects arising under normal use.
The warranty is valid in the UK and applies from the original purchase date at Nordic Shop Ltd.

3. WHAT THE WARRANTY DOES NOT COVER
The warranty does NOT cover the following:
- Damage caused by incorrect use or storage
- Water damage (unless the product is IP67/IP68-rated)
- Mechanical damage (drops, shocks, pressure)
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, battery natural capacity loss below 20%)
- Damage from unauthorised repair or modification
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect functionality
- Damage from power surges or incorrect voltage
